Bibbler

(Or, Scrabble-Boggle-Bejeweled)

This game was initially conceived by Lilly, DC, Vera, and myself, after we got bored of playing regular scrabble. A few weeks later, after the success of 2048, I decided to put it into motion with this game. With that being said, all the CSS magic is shamelessly stolen from 2048. Play my game. Tell me what you think of it. Yes, there are a lot of bugs (I will be working to fix as many as I can). In the meantime, enjoy!
